Project Overview

Project Description

The proposed web application is a promotional and informational website created for a small business owner named Bob. Bob runs a lawn care and landscaping service and wants a website that showcases his work, lists available services, helps potential customers learn more about his business, and provides a contact form for service requests. This website will help improve the visibility of Bob’s business, allow customers to easily view service examples, and simplify initial communication.

Intended Users

  • Local homeowners seeking lawn care or landscaping services
  • Potential new business clients
  • Returning customers looking for contact or service updates

Overview of Website Content

  • Business services and pricing description
  • Photo gallery showcasing completed projects
  • Contact form allowing users to reach Bob
  • Business background and mission

Site Map

Website Page Breakdown

1. Home Page (index.html)

  • Purpose: Introduce the business and provide quick navigation.
  • Users: All visitors.
  • Content: Business name, welcome message, hero image, intro text.
  • Interactivity: Buttons linking to Services and Gallery pages.

2. About Page (about.html)

  • Purpose: Explain Bob’s background and business mission.
  • Users: Potential customers.
  • Content: Text, business history, photo of Bob.
  • Interactivity: None besides navigation.

3. Services Page (services.html)

  • Purpose: List services and pricing estimates.
  • Users: Prospective customers evaluating service options.
  • Content: Service descriptions and pricing categories.
  • Interactivity: Accordion or tabs (jQuery UI) to show/hide details.

4. Gallery Page (gallery.html)

  • Purpose: Display before/after images of completed projects.
  • Users: Customers comparing quality.
  • Interactivity: JavaScript image slideshow or lightbox gallery.

5. Contact Page (contact.html)

  • Purpose: Allow users to send service inquiries.
  • Users: Anyone wanting to contact Bob.
  • Content: Name, phone/email, message form fields.
  • Validation: Required fields using JavaScript form validation.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Gallery Page: JavaScript slideshow/lightbox for images.
  • Services Page: jQuery UI Accordion to expand service descriptions.
  • Contact Page: JavaScript form validation, optional AJAX submit simulation.
